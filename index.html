Portal & Archives for Student Educative Records

A single platform to track and showcase every student's verified academic and co-curricular achievements in one place.

Get Started

Celebrating Achievements Beyond Grades.

We built this platform to showcase the amazing things students do outside the classroom whether it's co-curricular activities, workshops, research, or volunteering. These achievements are a big part of every student's journey, and through this project, we want to give them the spotlight they truly deserve.

How It Works

Students upload their academic records, the system generates AI-powered CVs, leaderboards track performance, professors access and connect with students, institutions generate analytics, and graduates move into the alumni network.
